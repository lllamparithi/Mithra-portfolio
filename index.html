About Me

Meet the voice behind thousands of captivating stories and the energy that lights up every event.

Hello, I'm Mithra.

As a well-experienced media professional, I bring a wealth of expertise from both Print and Broadcast Media. For years, I had the honor of being known as "Salem's Periya Papa" while hosting multiple shows at Suryan FM 93.9, where I connected with thousands of listeners and honed my craft as a storyteller and entertainer.

I am a JCI certified professional Public Speaker, a skilled Event Organizer, a soft skills trainer, and a passionate Motivational Speaker. My voice is not just a tool; it's a vehicle for bringing your brand's message to life.

With an MBA in HR & Marketing, I understand the strategic side of communication, ensuring every project is not only creative but also effective. I'm dedicated to delivering excellence and creating content that truly resonates.
